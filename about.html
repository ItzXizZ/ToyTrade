About

This website was created by Ethan, a
grade 8 St. Michaels University School student who, outside of school,
enjoys programming websites, participating in Model United Nations conferences, playing tennis
competitivley, and competing in + preparing for provincial math competitions.

The event that gave the inspiration for this website took place two years prior;
Ethan's mom had picked him up from school and volunteered to drop of some used toys to an in-need family.
Before hand, the children had chose the toys they wished to recieve, allowing for
a more direct personal experience to occur. Consequently, after dropping off the toys,
the joy present throughout the children's faces, while putting a smile on that of his own,
caused the idea for ToyTrade to form within Ethan's mind.

Ethan has numerous, more basic and less-aesthetically pleasing,
completed websites which he developed in prior years;
